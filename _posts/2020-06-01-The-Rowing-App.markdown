### Features


- Fleet, Rower, Lineup Management
- Workout logs
- Tools
	- Pace Calculator
	- VO2max Calculator
	- Stroke Rate Timer
	- Calorie Calculator
	- Weight Adjustment Calculator
	- Watts Converter
	- Multiple Stopwatches
